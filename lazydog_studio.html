Engineering highlights

Custom Cursor System
JavaScript · Snippet
// LazyDog Studios — Custom Cursor with Magnetic Labels
// Three-layer cursor: instant dot, eased ring, contextual label

const dot = document.querySelector('.cursor-dot');
const ring = document.querySelector('.cursor-ring');
let mouseX = 0, mouseY = 0, ringX = 0, ringY = 0;

document.addEventListener('mousemove', e => {
  mouseX = e.clientX;
  mouseY = e.clientY;
  dot.style.left = mouseX + 'px';
  dot.style.top = mouseY + 'px';
});

// Eased ring follow — creates magnetic tension
function animateRing() {
  ringX += (mouseX - ringX) * 0.15;
  ringY += (mouseY - ringY) * 0.15;
  ring.style.left = ringX + 'px';
  ring.style.top = ringY + 'px';
  requestAnimationFrame(animateRing);
}

Discovery

Every successful project begins with understanding. We take the time to learn about your business, audience, objectives, and vision. Through research and discussion, we establish a clear foundation that guides every decision moving forward. One week.
02

Strategy

With a solid understanding of your goals, we define the project's direction, structure, and creative approach. This phase ensures every element serves a purpose and aligns with your brand, audience, and long-term objectives. One week.

Design

We craft thoughtful digital experiences through carefully considered layouts, visual systems, and user interactions. Every detail is refined with clarity, usability, and aesthetics in mind, resulting in work that feels distinctive and purposeful. One to two weeks.

Development

Once the design is approved, we bring it to life through clean, reliable, and performance-focused development. Every project is built to be responsive, accessible, and ready for real-world use across modern devices and browsers. One to two weeks.

After final approval, we prepare your project for deployment and ensure a smooth transition from development to launch. We remain available for guidance, documentation, and post-launch assistance to help everything perform as intended. Launch preparation and deployment: up to three weeks. 14 — Frequently Asked Questions

Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know about LazyDogTemplates — templates, downloads, licensing, and more. If your question isn't here, write to us.

Are any templates free?
Only invoice templates (from the invoice generator section) are free. All other digital products are paid — including Pitch Decks, Media Kits, Resumes, Cover Letters, CVs, Website UI Kits, and Social Media Kits. Each is available as a one-time purchase with no subscription required.
How much do templates cost?
Prices vary depending on the template type, complexity, and design detail. Each product page displays its final price before purchase. All purchases are one-time payments with lifetime access to the downloaded file.
Do I need to create an account to purchase?
You can browse all templates without an account. To complete a purchase and receive your download, you'll need to provide your email — this is where we send the download link. Creating a free account lets you save favourites, track your orders, and re-download any template you've purchased.
Can I use these templates for commercial projects?
Yes. All templates are licensed for personal and commercial use. You can use them for client work, your own business, presentations, and job applications. You may not resell or redistribute the original template files themselves.
What formats are available?
Format depends on the template category. Career documents (resumes, CVs, cover letters) come as .DOCX + .PDF, editable in Microsoft Word and Google Docs. Pitch decks and media kits are available in PowerPoint (.pptx) and/or Canva. Each template page clearly lists all included file formats before purchase.
Are the career document templates ATS-friendly?
Yes. Our resume and CV templates are built specifically to pass Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS). We use clean single-column layouts, ATS-safe fonts, no tables or graphics in the body, and standard section headings — so your resume gets seen by a human, not filtered out by software.
Can I edit and customise the templates?
Absolutely — that's the whole point. Every template is fully editable. Change colours, fonts, text, and layout to match your brand or personal style. Career document templates open directly in Microsoft Word or Google Docs with no special software needed.
How do I receive my purchase after buying?
After completing your purchase, a download link is sent to your email address instantly. If you don't see it within a few minutes, check your spam folder. You can also re-download any purchased template at any time from your account page.
What industries do your templates cover?
We cover 20+ industries including Tech & AI, Medical & Healthcare, Finance, Construction, Retail, Education, Real Estate, and more. Use the filter on any template category page to find designs specific to your industry.
How do I request a custom template?
We offer custom template requests in select cases. These are separate from our standard digital products. You can email us your requirements and we will confirm availability and pricing.
