LD

← Back to Journal

Pitch Decks is where LazyDogTemplates began. It's our first section, and right now, it's intentionally not full.

What you'll find here today is a small set of sample decks — not thousands of templates, not every industry covered, not a wall of options to scroll through. That's on purpose.

Why We're Not Racing to Add More

It would be easy to flood this section with hundreds of decks overnight. It wouldn't be easy to make sure every one of them is actually good, and actually easy to find.

We'd rather get that part right first.

A library with 2,000 templates isn't useful if a user has to dig through all of them to find the one that fits. That's not a feature — that's just more scrolling. Our aim isn't to be the biggest collection out there. It's to make sure that when someone lands here, they find the right deck quickly, without guessing.

What We're Actually Building Right Now

Behind the scenes, we're working on a proper search and guidance system — something that understands what a user actually needs and points them straight to it, instead of leaving them to sift through options on their own. Tone, industry, purpose, structure — the goal is a search that actually understands the request, not just matches keywords.

That work matters more to us right now than adding deck number 51. Once it's solid, growing the library becomes worth doing — because it'll actually be easy to navigate, not just bigger.

What This Means for You, Right Now

If you're browsing Pitch Decks today, you're seeing an early, honest snapshot — a handful of quality samples while we build the system that'll make browsing genuinely easy. We'd rather show you that than pretend to be finished.

More categories, more decks, and a much smarter way to find them are all coming. This is just the starting point.
